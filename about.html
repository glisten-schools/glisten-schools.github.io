About Glisten

Two schools under one ownership, serving children at different stages with care, purpose, and strong values.

Our Story

A learning community built around growth, care, and excellence.

Glisten Schools brings together Glisten Preparatory and Glisten Pre and Primary School in Mirerani. Both schools share the motto Together We Excel and a commitment to purposeful learning, strong character, confidence, and responsible growth.

Glisten Preparatory nurtures early learners in Baby Class, Middle Class, and Pre-Unit. Glisten Pre and Primary School serves pupils from Standard One to Standard Seven. The two schools operate under the same ownership and are located approximately 2 km apart.

We believe education should prepare children not only for examinations, but also for respectful, responsible, confident, and caring participation in society.
