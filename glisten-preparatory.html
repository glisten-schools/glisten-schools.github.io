Head Teacher's Welcome

Every child is valued, cared for, and encouraged to grow.

Welcome to Glisten Preparatory, where every child is valued, cared for, and encouraged to grow with confidence.

We believe that the early years form an important foundation for a child’s future. Through purposeful classroom learning, play, songs, creativity, discipline, and positive social interaction, we help our pupils develop academically, emotionally, physically, and socially.

Our teachers are committed to providing a safe, loving, and stimulating environment where children can discover their abilities, build confidence, and enjoy learning. We warmly welcome parents to visit us and learn more about the opportunities available in Baby Class, Middle Class, and Pre-Unit.
